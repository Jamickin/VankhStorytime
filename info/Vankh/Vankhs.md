# Vankhs

Vankhs are the central storytelling points in the novel. They were created by [[Nankh'U]] to stabilise [[Experiment 2]]. There are 4 in existence, although the [[Proto-Humans]]-kind believe there to be hundreds if not thousands. 

They emit their characteristic [[Vankh Pulse]], a celestial vibration that governs the universe in many ways. 

[[Nankh'U]] created the last [[Prime Vankh]] to imprison [[Mald'Ra]] within. This is merely a narrative device, however.
