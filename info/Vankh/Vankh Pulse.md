# Vankh Pulse

A Vankh-Pulse is the phenomena cause by the pitch black spheres that dot space in the universe of [[Experiment 2]]. It is characterised by the enormous sound and rattling shockwave that travels with its coming. [[Proto-Human]]s have adapted to this, structuring their culture, time and technology around it.

A [[Vankh-Sett]] is a example of this, as it represents a full rotation of the [[Vankhs]].

[[FTL]] travel is impossible without the [[Vankhs]].

Physiologically, [[Proto-Human]]s are adapted to this by means of their eyes, muscles, tendons and mind.

The [[Proto-Human]] culture is also structured around the [[Vankh Pulse]], creating a strong sense of community within all aswell as strife.
