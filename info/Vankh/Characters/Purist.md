# Name:

- Purists
---
# Characteristics:

- Conservative faction, they would attempt to rid the universe of technology
- Led by [[The Prophet]]
- Bear marks and scars of augmentations that have been removed, either willingly or by force
- Directly oppose [[Cicillian]] 
---

# Weaknesses:

- Weak sense of purpose, they cling to the word of [[The Prophet]]
- Ban technology, still use it. Thus they have no expertise
---

# Role:

- Act as the co-operator in the themes of [[Correspondence]], [[Polarity]] and [[Cause and Effect]]
- Offer the pinnacle moment in the Epilogue where Pro comes into contact with the [[Vankhs]]
