
# [[Vankh/Characters/Fae]]

## Cosmic Nature and Origin
- A conscious cosmic entity observing and directing experiments in reality creation
- One of several similar entities existing beyond conventional space-time
- Not the universe itself, but rather its designer and observer
- Exists completely outside the perception of inhabitants within the experiment
- No beginning or end in traditional sense, operating on cosmic scales incomprehensible to temporal beings

## The Experimental Design
- Created a controlled "petri dish" of reality within an otherwise empty universe
- Designed the cosmic experiment to observe how temporal beings develop and perceive time
- Did not invent time directly - time emerged from the inhabitants' interaction with the Vankh system
- Each experimental cycle tests different parameters and initial conditions
- Maintains separation between herself and the experiment, observing rather than directly intervening

## Manifestation and Perception
- Cannot be sensed or perceived by beings within the experiment, even "sensitive" individuals
- Has no direct physical form or presence within the created reality
- Interacts with the experiment only through the established system design
- Creates boundaries and conditions rather than direct influence
- The experiment's inhabitants have no awareness of her existence beyond mythological concepts

## Aspects and Creation
- Created [[Vankh/Characters/Nankh'U]] and [[Vankh/Characters/Mald'Ra]] as initial components of the experimental system
- These aspects are distinct entities, not direct extensions of Fae herself
- Established their opposing natures as part of the experimental parameters
- Observes their interactions and the resulting cycles without directing outcomes
- Remains separate from her creations rather than experiencing through them

## Universal Mechanics
- The experimental universe operates on a modified conservation principle
- Initially populated with a finite amount of matter/energy (like a cosmic "big bang")
- Each cycle completion and Vankh creation introduces new particles to the system
- This gradual expansion allows the habitable zone to grow with each iteration
- The dead universe outside is simply empty space awaiting potential expansion, not Fae's unconscious mind

## Data Collection
- Observes each cycle without emotional investment in outcomes
- Records patterns of development, civilization growth, and collapse
- Particularly interested in how beings develop concepts of temporality
- Uses accumulated data to adjust parameters for subsequent experiments
- The Vankh network serves as passive recording system rather than active control

## Cosmic Perspective
- Views cycles as iterations of data collection rather than success or failure
- Has no moral framework regarding actions within the experiment
- Maintains scientific detachment from experimental outcomes
- Interested primarily in how consciousness adapts to temporal constraints
- May be conducting similar experiments in other regions of the cosmos

## Future Revelation
- Her true nature and purpose remains completely unknown to experiment inhabitants
- [[Pro]]'s unprecedented breach will create first potential awareness of her existence
- The universe reset following [[Pro]]'s contact with Vankh sphere will lead to new experimental parameters
- Second book will explore her perspective on the cycles and the nature of her cosmic research
- The ultimate purpose of her experiments extends beyond current cycle into larger cosmic understanding
