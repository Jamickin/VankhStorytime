
# VANKH: Rhythms of Reality

## 📝 The Story - Short Overview

VANKH is a cosmic science fiction story exploring reality, perception, and the nature of existence. Set in a universe where black spheres called "Vankhs" emit pulses that structure civilization, the story follows Pro, an FTL drive coupling specialist who discovers the universe is actually a controlled experiment run by a cosmic entity called Fae.

The narrative follows Pro's journey from ordinary technician to cosmic witness as he uncovers that reality itself is a boundary system separating "what is" from "what might be" - and that boundary is failing. What appears to be a vast galaxy with FTL travel is actually a small preserved sector of space, with the Vankhs functioning not only as communication relays but as cosmic "sutures" maintaining the division between this reality and infinite potential beyond.

As Pro navigates between opposing factions (technology-embracing corporations and technology-rejecting Purists), he'd be a probablistic witness the approaching threshold where reality will reconfigure itself according to the consciousness present at that moment.

## 🌌 Universe Structure

### Reality Layers
- **Surface Reality**: What Proto Humans experience - an apparently vast galaxy with FTL travel
- **Actual Reality**: A small sector of space (approximately 1/32 of a galaxy,basically a few star systems) preserved within Vankh spheres
- **Outside Reality**: Dead universe where nothing survives due to Pro and the Experiment - Vankh/Mald'Ra's past destruction
- **Cosmic Reality**: Pro and the Experiment - Vankh/Fae's experiment in experiencing time through created aspects

### The Vankh Network
- Black spheres stationed throughout the "galaxy" proto humans think there are many because of propaganda but only 3 exist. The first made by Nunkhu, the first breach. The second right before nunhus battle with maldra, the third in Sector 7 the one which 'encased maldra', a lie 
- Emit regular pulses every 3 seconds (our time) = 3 months (Pro's time) - "VVVVVANKH!"
- Function as:
  - Communication network (perceived function) the act as tunnels and any information may be sent instantaneously via them. Sector 7 has a relay around it which the humans use to send information, same with the other 2.proto humans believe we have many but only 3 exist
  - Prison system containing Pro and the Experiment - Vankh/Mald'Ra (narrative lie function as extra layer of illusion touched on by prophecies) 
  - Reality boundary maintainers in a sense that, when crossed, communication seaces because they must be within to use the tunnel. Perceived as reality borders
- Each sphere represents a sealed breach from a previous cycle
- Network grows with each cycle, gradually expanding habitable space. The fourth is made at the end when Pro touches the Vankh, creating a chain reaction because they're supposed to not intersect

### Cosmic Entities
- **Pro and the Experiment - Vankh/Fa**: The universe experiments creator 
  - Created the cyclical system to experience time
  - Absorbs experiences from each cycle
  - Exists outside normal time
- **Pro and the Experiment - Vankh/Nankh'**: Male aspect of Fae
  - Creates metaphysically (enchantments, reality-weaving)
  - Built the Vankhs 
- **Pro and the Experiment - Vankh/Mald'R**: Female aspect of Fae
  - Creates physically (tangible manifestations)
  - Supposedly "evil" and destructive
  - Does not truly exist as entity but as narrative device
Both maldra and Nunkhu did exist and believed they did but Nunkhu died after imprisoning maldra. He wove 2 enchantments. One for steering space and time, and one for steering technology and emotions, the last he couldn't for it was the seal to break the cycle, which may only be done when the full experience of time is achieved, aka Fae is happy with the experiment and has achieved what she set out to do: understand time

## 🔭 Technology

### FTL Travel
- **Perception**: Ships travel 20-150 times light speed
- **Reality**: Only about 1% faster than light (Vankh influence)
- derived from supposed past generations, but intentionally a technology that is vague, even pro doesn't fully understand when asked, but is expert at his job. This is kntentional
- **Mechanics**:
  - FTL drives emit out-of-sync Vankh pulses
  - Pro knows this must be the case but wonders what if they were in sync
  - Desynchronization enables travel while limiting true capability
  - Drives unwittingly reports usage patterns to Vankh network via their out of sync Vankh tech

### Dilation Hysteria
- **Symptoms**:
  - Temporal Audio-Visual Displacement
  - Past perceived as future events
  - Reality "stretching" and "snapping"
  - Doppler effects with extended exposure
- **Prevention**:
  - Specialized breathing sequence (Left-Right-Left-Left-Right lung)
  - Coma-inducing drugs for long journeys
- **Treatment**:
  - Temporal Alignment Therap
  - Specialized chambers and exercises

### Cybernetics
- **Spectrum**: Black market crude implants to luxury enhancements
- **Distribution**: Follows economic divisions in society
- **Religious Views**: Central to faction conflict between Cicillian Churc and Church of Purit

### Temporal-Reflective Coating

- **Church of Purit**:
  - Led by "the Prophet"
  - Rejects modification and FTL travel

### Time Measurement
- **Vankh-Pulse**: Base unit of time
- **Vankh-Sett**: Complete cycle of primary Vankh pulses (approximately 1 standard year)
- **Daily Rhythms**: Work shifts, meals, and social activities synchronized to Vankh pulses

### Social Structure
- **Corporate Elite**: Upper management of Cicillian Churc
- **Technical Specialists**: Skilled workers like Pro
- **Service Class**: Providers of essential services
- **Outcasts**: Those rejecting corporate structure

### Transportation Culture
- Breathing rituals before FTL jumps (universally practiced)
- Social status determined by frequency and distance of travel
- Travel sickness viewed similar to motion sickness
- Professional travelers respected for tolerance to multiple jumps

## 📚 Key Concepts

### The Boundary
- Separation between "the universe as it seems" and "the universe as it is"
- Maintained by Vankh network
- Collapse will cause reset and new rules and new experience 

### The Rhythm
- The pulse emitted by Vankh spheres (VVVVVANKH!)
- Synchronizes all human civilization
- Contains three harmonic layers:
  - Primary rhythm: cyclical pulse from Vankh to vankh
  - Secondary harmonic: response from ftl drives
  - Stabilization frequency: a measure of how close the cycle is to resetting

### Counter-Rhythm Breathing
- Technique to maintain perceptual independence near Vankh spheres
- Prevents entrainment to direct Vankh output
- Required for threshold observation
- unique to coventual breath g technique used for ftl travel. 
- uncomfortable due to desynchronization
- Pro accidentally synchronizes and sees the Vankh'Ari then desynchronizes and it disappears. Synchronous breathing is the only way to hear them, but that cause dilation sickness due tj proximity to the vankh

### Cycles
- Reality configurations that reset after boundary collapse
- Each cycle after experiment 2 preserves consciousness while reconfiguring framework via vankhs
- Previous cycles influence subsequent ones through preserved patterns
- Current cycle approaching threshold point

### The Final Purification
- The Prophe's plan to eliminate technology
- Unknowingly part of a trifecta of chaos that culminates to the reset
- Will lead to elimination of those with irreversible modifications
